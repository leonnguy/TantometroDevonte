Biography

Reggae and dancehall singer and deejay duo Tanto Metro & Devonte have teamed up with award-winning producers Sly & Robbie for their fourth studio album Sly and Robbie presents Tanto Metro & Devonte. According to a press release, their aim is to reclaim the top of the charts. “We were far more involved in the creation of this album than any of the previous ones. We were involved musically, creatively, emotionally and spiritually. Plus, we have not released an album for a while now, so this is like our first album, all over again,” states the duo. The album includes ten tracks and is a combination of fun, love, and inspiration. The duo’s favorite cut is a remake of Joe Higgs‘ classic There’s a Reward. “The message of hope in this track resonates deeply within us. Our journey has been very challenging thus far but we know that ‘There’s a Reward’ for us, and it’s gonna be great,” explains the duo, and concludes: “We wanted to make a universally happy album. So the world can sing, dance, love and be inspired.”
